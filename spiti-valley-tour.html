SPITI VALLEY TOUR PACKAGE

Trip Itinerary

Day 0 : Depart from Delhi around 9:30 PM

Day 1 : Reach Shimla. Travel to Rakcham/Chitkul. Stay at chitkul or Rakcham

Day 2 : Rakcham/Chitkul to Nako (Nako Lake covering same afternoon/evening). Stay at Tabo.

Day 3 : Tabo monastery. Visit Tabo Monastery then drive towards Kaza while covering Dhankar monastery. Stay at Kaza.

Day 4 : Kaza - Langza - Komik - Hikkim - Kaza. Stay at Kaza.

Day 5 : Kaza - Key monastery- Chicham - Kibber - Kaza. Stay at Kaza.
Day 6 : K Kaza to Kalpa. Leisure time under the Kinnaur Himalayas and market hopping. Stay at Kalpa.

Day 7 : Kalpa to Shimla drop. Board bus towards Delhi.

Day 8 : Reach Delhi in the morning
