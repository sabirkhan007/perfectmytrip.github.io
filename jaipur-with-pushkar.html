Jaipur With Pushkar

Trip Itinerary

Day 1 :
  • On arrival at Jaipur railway station or airport, get a transfer to the hotel
  • Check-in to the hotel and relax
  • In the evening, you can take an optional tour to visit Laxminarayan Temple (Birla Mandir) and Chokhi Dhani, an ethnic village resort
  • Return to the hotel
  • Overnight stay in Jaipur

Day 2 :
  • After breakfast, start for a full day local sightseeing tour in Jaipur
  • Visit Amber Fort and Palace, take a photo stop at Jal Mahal, City Palace and Museum, Jantar Mantar (observatory) and Hawa Mahal
  • Spend the evening at leisure
  • Overnight stay in Jaipur

Day 3 :
  • After breakfast, start for a full-day excursion to Ajmer and Pushkar
  • On arrival, visit Ajmer-e-Sharief Dargah, Adhai-Din-Ka Jhonpara, Ana Sagar Lake
  • Drive to Pushkar, visit various temples and Ghats and holy Pushkar Lake
  • There are more than 400 hundred temples in Pushkar but the main attraction is the temple of Lord Brahma
  • Return to the hotel in Jaipur

Day 4 : Thimphu - Paro
  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel
  • Get a transfer to Jaipur railway station or airport for your onward journey
